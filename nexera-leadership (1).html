Validated Assessments

Four Instruments.
One Integrated Picture.

Nexera holds active licensing for four of the most rigorously validated leadership instruments in the world. Each one feeds automatically into the leader's intelligence report — no coordination burden on your school.

"
Personality data without context is just a personality test. Nexera integrates every instrument into a coaching narrative your leaders can actually use.
Hogan
Hogan Assessments · Licensed Partner

HPI + HDS + MVPI

The gold standard in personality assessment for leaders. Measures everyday strengths, derailment risks under pressure, and core values that drive behavior.

  • HPI — Bright side: how leaders appear to others at their best
  • HDS — Dark side: derailment risks that surface under stress
  • MVPI — Values: what motivates decisions
  • Direct link sent by Nexera — no school coordination needed
DISC
DISC Assessment · Licensed Partner

Behavioral Profile

The most widely used behavioral assessment in the world. Identifies how leaders respond — communication style, decision pace, team dynamics under pressure.

  • D — Dominance: responding to problems and challenges
  • I — Influence: persuading others to your point of view
  • S — Steadiness: adapting to pace of environment
  • Used alongside 360° for leadership style context
Gallup
CliftonStrengths · Licensed Partner

CliftonStrengths 34

Identifies what a leader naturally does best — and gives a shared language for the whole team to understand and leverage each other's strengths.

  • Top 5 Themes — the strengths leaders lead from daily
  • Full 34 Themes — complete picture of potential + patterns
  • Team Grid — how leadership team strengths complement each other
  • Existing school data accepted — no retake required
360°
Nexera Multi-Rater Feedback · Proprietary

Leadership 360° Feedback

Structured multi-rater feedback from supervisors, peers, and direct reports. Mapped to leadership competencies and renewal benchmarks — not just generic traits.

  • Self score vs. others score — the gap is the coaching agenda
  • Competency breakdown: culture building, communication, talent development
  • Values alignment signal: does how they lead match what they say they value?
  • Year-over-year tracking — movement from baseline shown each cycle
Culture Survey

A research-backed, longitudinal culture diagnostic with campus-level breakdowns, eNPS reporting, and values-alignment domains. Tracks 8 domains across multiple cycles — giving leaders a measurable read on whether your values are being lived, not just declared.

The Three Cost Drivers We Model
① Leader Replacement Cost
When a principal or network leader leaves, replacement costs an average of $22,000–$35,000 — including search, interview process, onboarding, and the 6–12 month productivity lag before a new hire reaches full effectiveness. Source: NAESP / RAND research on principal turnover.
② Renewal Funding Risk
A 3-year renewal instead of 5-year — or a probationary renewal — creates enrollment uncertainty worth $12–$18 per student per year in planning capacity. For a 500-student school, that's $60K–$90K in avoidable risk per year of uncertainty.
③ Development ROI
Leaders in structured, data-driven coaching programs show measurably stronger retention, culture, and student outcomes within 12–24 months. We count every leader in active coaching as a protected investment — not just a cost line.
